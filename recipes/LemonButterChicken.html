Lemon Butter Chicken

Description

The lemon butter chicken dish is a culinary delight that tantalizes the taste buds with its
exquisite flavors and textures. As you take your first bite, you'll be greeted by the succulent
and tender chicken pieces, each coated in a luscious lemon-infused buttery sauce.

The taste is a perfect balance of tanginess from the lemon and richness from the butter,
creating a harmonious symphony of flavors. The savory gravy seeps into the chicken, enhancing
its juiciness and adding depth to every mouthful. The combination of the zesty lemon and creamy
butter creates a delightful contrast that is both refreshing and comforting.

This dish is a culinary masterpiece for several reasons. Firstly, the citrusy notes from the lemon
brighten up the overall flavor profile, providing a refreshing and palate-cleansing sensation.
Secondly, the creamy butter sauce adds a luxurious richness that coats each piece of chicken,
making it incredibly indulgent. The chicken itself is cooked to perfection, maintaining its tenderness
and juiciness, making it a delight to savor.

Whether you are a fan of poultry or simply a food enthusiast, this lemon butter chicken dish
offers a satisfying and unforgettable dining experience. It is an ideal choice for those seeking
a combination of tangy, creamy, and savory flavors that harmonize beautifully. Whether enjoyed on its own,
paired with aromatic rice, or accompanied by fresh vegetables, this dish is sure to leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Paprika
  • Coriander Powder
  • Lemon Juice
  • Butter
  • Dried Parsley
  • Black Pepper
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Oil

Steps

    Cut chicken into strips
    Mix and Marinate Chicken for 1 hour with 1tbsp of each Ingredients below:
    Black Pepper, Salt , Garlic Powder , Chilli Flakes ,
    Paprika , Coriander Powder , lemon juice , dark soy sauce, Oil
    Make Lemon Butter Sauce
    2 tbsp Butter, Juice of 1 Lemon, 1 teaspoon Chilli Flakes,
    2 teaspoon Dried Parsley, 1 teaspoon Black Pepper
    Mix them all
    If using wooden skewer, soak in hot water for 30 min.
    Skewer Chicken
    Cook in Air Fryer or BBQ or Oven or Pan Cook
    Air Fryer and Oven Cook at 390 C for 10 minutes flapping half way through
    Coat the Butter Sauce on Strip Chicken
